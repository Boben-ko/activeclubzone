Welcome to Active Club Zone

Your go-to platform for club organization, team creation, and event management.

Explore Features

Features

Club Organization

Manage your club with ease. Set up club name, logo, country, city, and publish club news.

Team Creation

Invite players to join your team, even unregistered ones via a link. Keep track of members' ratings.

Event Calendar

Schedule recreational events, set deadlines for sign-ups, and keep everyone in the loop.

Balanced Team Generation

Automatically create balanced teams based on players' ratings. Choose the number of teams and players per team.

Personalized Dashboards

Get customized dashboards for Managers, Players, and Manager-Players with essential data at a glance.

Push Notifications

Stay up to date with important notifications for events, team invites, and updates.

Polls and Feedback

Managers can create polls to rate players and confirm attendance after each event.

Google Maps Integration

Easily add event locations with Google Maps integration for a seamless experience.

Comments and Discussions

Members can leave comments on events and manager posts to facilitate discussions.
